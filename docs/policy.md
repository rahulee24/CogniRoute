# Corporate Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Overview
This refund policy governs all software licenses purchased directly from our site.

## Monthly Plans
- Standard Pro monthly subscriptions ($49/month) can be canceled at any time.
- Cancellation requests take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for mid-month cancellations.

## Annual Plans
- Standard Team annual licenses ($1,200/year) and Pro annual plans are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase.
- Refund requests after 14 days will not be accepted.

## Enterprise Plans
- Enterprise plans ($4,999/year or custom pricing) are subject to individual contract terms.
- Enterprise contracts generally require a 30-day notice for termination-for-cause.
